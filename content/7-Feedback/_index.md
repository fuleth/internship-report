### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is highly professional and supportive. The mentors and the admin team are always willing to help whenever I encounter difficulties, and my fellow interns are open to discussing and sharing knowledge throughout the learning and working process.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentors encourage independent learning and problem-solving, motivating interns to explore new knowledge on their own while gaining hands-on experience through real projects. The admin team is always available to provide support when needed, regularly organizes community meetings to share technical knowledge, and offers valuable feedback on project architecture.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The tasks assigned to me complemented the cybersecurity knowledge I learned at university while also exposing me to cloud technologies, an area I previously had limited opportunities to explore. This allowed me to strengthen my fundamental knowledge and develop practical, industry-related skills.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I gained many new skills, including working with AWS services, collaborating effectively in a team, and communicating professionally in a corporate environment while designing an enterprise-scale simulation project.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is very positive. Everyone treats each other with respect, works professionally, and maintains a friendly atmosphere. When working on projects, team members are always willing to support one another and provide constructive feedback regardless of their roles. This made me feel like a valued member of the team, even as an intern.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
One of the greatest strengths of the internship program is that it provides interns with opportunities to participate in various academic and technical activities, including workshops, community events, hands-on labs, and real-world projects. If possible, the program could be further improved by providing a clearer roadmap for each stage of the internship, making it easier for interns to track their progress.

### Additional Questions
- The aspect I appreciated the most during my internship was the opportunity to meet and connect with leading professionals in the cloud industry, as well as experienced experts in the field of Information Technology.
- One area where I believe the program could be improved is the approval process for working at the office.
- I would definitely recommend this internship program to my friends because it provides an excellent environment for anyone who wants to start learning about cloud computing, AWS, and practical, hands-on approaches to technology.

### Suggestions & Expectations
- I hope the program can increase the number of available office working opportunities so that interns have more chances to work on-site.
- I also hope to have the opportunity to participate in similar activities in the future, especially Community Day events, where I can continue learning about new technologies and industry trends.
